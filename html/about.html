Nana Ioseliani AKA nana.ios is a multidisciplinary producer and musician based in Berlin. Drawing from her diverse musical background and a deep love for experimentation, Nana has developed her solo project into an exploration of sound and art.

Her early exposure to Traditional Polyphonic Singing profoundly influenced her understanding of the voice as a powerful instrument of self-expression. Her passion for music expanded beyond singing, leading her to explore various art forms and mediums, including fashion, dance and film.

Nana assumes complete independence as she writes, records, and produces her own music. Her work extends beyond traditional music production, encompassing a wide range of mediums such as imagery, mixed media, and sonic landscapes. From crafting costumes to directing her own music videos, Nana embraces a multidisciplinary approach, pushing the boundaries of artistic expression.

Her involvement in Berlin's vibrant artistic community has played a pivotal role in her growth as an artist. Engaging with fellow creatives, organizing events, and collaborating on projects have provided her with invaluable opportunities for self-discovery and artistic exploration.

“ As an artist, it is important for me to connect with other creatives in the industry, as I believe that together we can achieve more than we can imagine. Throughout my artistic journey, I have discovered that I learn the most from people, and the choice of sharing knowledge is truly empowering. This is what I cherish about Berlin the most - the abundance of creative individuals who foster a sense of community rather than competition, where egos dissolve and connections are formed to contribute and collaborate.”

Her debut album, "The Way of Joy," weaves a captivating tapestry of ethereal pop, fusing elements of synth, dream, and experimental pop genres, guiding listeners through an innocent, delightful, and emotionally rich journey of self-exploration and boundless creativity.
